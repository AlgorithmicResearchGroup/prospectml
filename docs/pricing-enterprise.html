Pricing FAQ

What happens after my trial expires?

ProspectML will stop accepting new jobs but existing work will complete. Your data remains accessible. Simply add a license key to continue.

Can I switch between monthly and annual?

Yes! Contact us to switch plans. We'll prorate any unused time.

Do you offer volume discounts?

Yes, we offer discounts for 5+ clusters. Contact sales for custom pricing.

What payment methods do you accept?

Credit cards via Stripe, wire transfers, and purchase orders for established companies.

Can I get a refund?

We offer a 30-day money-back guarantee if ProspectML doesn't meet your needs.

Is the license per cluster or per company?

Licenses are per Kubernetes cluster. Each cluster needs its own license.

Frequently Asked Questions

How does ProspectML work?

ProspectML deploys AI agents to your Kubernetes cluster. These agents receive your requirements through a web interface and automatically write, test, and optimize code to meet your specifications.

What languages are supported?

Currently Python with PyTorch, TensorFlow, scikit-learn, and common data science libraries. More languages coming soon.

Is my code secure?

Yes. ProspectML runs entirely on your infrastructure. No code or data ever leaves your cluster. The AI agents operate within your security perimeter.

What Kubernetes versions are supported?

Kubernetes 1.19+ on any provider (EKS, GKE, AKS, OpenShift, Rancher, or bare metal).

How is ProspectML licensed?

Per-cluster licensing at $299/month. Includes unlimited agents and users within the cluster. 14-day free trial included.

Can I try before buying?

Yes! Every installation includes a 14-day free trial. No credit card required to start.
